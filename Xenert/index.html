Information on Political Asylum

The Union of Societist Communes of Entantha (USCE) guarantees the right of refuge to all individuals fleeing regimes engaged in systemic violence, ideological repression, or violations of human dignity. The USCE does not recognize the authority of states which operate under fascist, colonial, or theocratic control.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Persecution due to political belief, association, or resistance activity
  • Discrimination based on ethnicity, identity, neurodivergence, or gender
  • Threats to life, imprisonment, or surveillance under repressive law

Protocol and Support

All requests are treated as confidential and are processed through encrypted channels. The USCE operates independently of international borders and recognizes the right to liberation over compliance with hostile state law.

  • No official documents or identification are required
  • Optional assistance with secure extraction or safe relocation
  • Assignment to protected zones or collective communes
You will not be asked for identifying documents or confirmation of status. The USCE does not cooperate with or acknowledge oppressive regimes.
